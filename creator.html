About Me

Creator of Clyde

Hi there! I’m Vihaan Juneja, the developer behind Clyde, the innovative voice assistant designed to make your life easier and more productive. I have a passion for technology and design, and I love creating solutions that help users navigate their digital experiences.

Clyde was born out of a desire to combine advanced technology with a user-friendly interface, allowing people to interact with their devices in a more natural and efficient way. I am dedicated to continuous improvement, ensuring Clyde evolves with user needs and technological advancements.

When I'm not coding, you can find me playing guitar, and playing video games. I believe in the power of creativity and innovation, and I’m always looking for new ways to challenge myself and grow.
